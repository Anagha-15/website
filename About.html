About

Settl application is an effective solution for reducing human intervention in receipt recognition and handling field. By reducing human intervention we can increase the integrity of bill processing. Hence increasing the dependability.Here we use deep learning and OCR methodologies to read and process receipts. Receipts can be exported to various forms like spreadsheet,documents etc. It is a simple application intended to make expense management quicker and easier. This allows a system which reduces human effort involved,producing results that are dependable,not error prone,easily reproducable.
